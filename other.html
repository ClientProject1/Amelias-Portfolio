Other Work

My dissertation was titled “The relationship between the fashion gatekeepers and the fashion influencers in western society”.

I enjoy writing and have written several essays during my time at university. I have also completed projects based on marketing strategies, brand communication, buying and merchandising and fashion research.

I am grateful to have had the opportunity to develop my knowledge and practice of roles within the fashion industry, such as journalism, photography and styling. It has allowed me to experiment and really work on my strengths, as well as boost my confidence and prepare me to begin my career.
